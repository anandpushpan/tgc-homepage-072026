Our History.

Gateway was founded on a simple, enduring conviction: that true technological value is engineered through deep relationships and reliable delivery. What began as a focused engineering firm has evolved into a global powerhouse, an orchestrator of digital ecosystems, and a strategic partner to ambitious companies worldwide.

Over nearly three decades, we have consistently anticipated shift points in technology, transitioning seamlessly from local software delivery to multinational engineering, from pure-play digital transformation to building and investing in the next generation of enterprises. Today, Gateway combines engineering DNA with an operator-first philosophy, building scalable businesses and delivering sustainable social impact.

Our Journey

01
1997 – 2000

The Beginning of Trust

Gateway began with a simple belief: to build technology people could rely on. Every project strengthened relationships, laying the foundation for an enterprise that would grow through trust rather than rapid expansion.

Focus & Transformation: Establishing core offshore software engineering capabilities and building foundational client trust.

Trust became our first product.

2000 – 2008

Crossing Borders

This era marked Gateway’s strategic expansion into the US and European markets. A critical milestone occurred in 2000 with the establishment of NINtec in the Netherlands, forging a powerful, long-term synergy that combined local, nearshore European presence with robust offshore delivery excellence.

Focus & Transformation: Scaling global delivery models, expanding European operations via NINtec, and embedding local teams across international borders.

Global by presence. Local by commitment.

2008 – 2016

Engineering Without Borders

Engineering capabilities expanded across continents, enabling seamless delivery from multiple technology hubs while maintaining one cohesive engineering culture.

Focus & Transformation: Scaling multi-country technology hubs across North America, Europe, and Asia, unifying cross-border engineering DNA.

One engineering DNA across many countries.

2016 – 2019

Reinvention Through Digital

Recognizing the market shift, Gateway and NINtec consolidated and rebranded as a unified digital entity. Concurrently, the ecosystem laid its first public market foundation with an initial listing on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) as an MSME, while heavily developing niche automotive and logistics platforms.

Focus & Transformation: Consolidating regional brands into a unified digital engineering powerhouse; establishing public market roots and transitioning into a digital transformation partner.

We evolved before the market demanded it.

2020 – 2023

Building an Ecosystem & Public Listing

This period marked the transition from delivering technology to creating businesses, driven by the institutional framework of our investment arm, TGC Capital Partners.

Focus & Transformation: Diversifying from IT services to enterprise building, venture backing, and achieving a major corporate milestone via Main Board public market listings on both BSE and NSE.

We started building companies, not just solutions.

2024 – 2025

Innovation Through Partnerships

Gateway expanded into emerging technologies through strategic partnerships with ambitious founders across Europe, transitioning from traditional software frameworks to cognitive and experiential solutions.

Focus & Transformation: Co-creating platforms in AI, healthcare, corporate communications, event technology, and digital experiences.

Every partnership expands the ecosystem for everyone. Operators first. Investors second.
